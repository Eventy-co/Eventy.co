About Us

Eventy.co offers a curated collection of custom Instagram filters designed to elevate the visual branding and aesthetic of events and celebrations. Their filters allow event hosts and attendees to seamlessly integrate a cohesive visual theme across social media platforms, with a variety of styles from elegant and romantic to modern and minimalist. Each filter is meticulously crafted to enhance photos and videos, transforming ordinary moments into shareable, on-brand content that helps create a polished, professional look and feel that resonates with guests. The filters are easy to apply and can be used by anyone with an Instagram account, providing a simple way for event attendees to get in on the branding action and foster a sense of community around the occasion.
